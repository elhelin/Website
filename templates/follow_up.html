Key Takeaways

The Experience

Cycling is simple, yet connects many problems and challenges of contemporary cities. It is intertwined with urban life in all its richness and complexity.

Location

Bike around the city's streets, canals, and bike lanes to various spots in Amsterdam and observe and experience how cycling is woven into urban life.

Network

Providing a fertile ground for sharing knowledge and learning about urban cycling, participating in a bike scavenger hunt with urban planners creates new networking opportunities, fostering connections with professionals.

Thank you for participating!

We appreciate your participation in the scavenger hunt! We hope you've had a chance to experience the simplicity of biking, which links so many issues and difficulties in modern cities. It is entangled with urban life in all its intricacy.

Read on if you want to learn more about how you can contribute to the change by using a multidisciplinary strategy to comprehend the complex causes and effects of urban cycling.

What's next?

The Netherlands is frequently cited as an ideal living environment because cycling has maintained a sizable portion of the country's mobility. Internationally renowned researchers in a variety of academic fields are also coming to understand this more and more.

Check out the Urban Cycling Institutes course on Coursera called "Unravelling the Cycling City" for more information, and establish relationships with experts and people who share your interests to learn more.
