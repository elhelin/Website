A. Is there residential/housing available nearby:
little / medium / a lot

B. Are there other services or amenities nearby:
little / medium / a lot

C. Is there a transit stop nearby:
yes / no / can’t see one

D. Is there a bike path nearby:
yes / no / can’t see one

E. Is there an underground car parking garage available:
yes / no

F. Count how many parking places are available for:
a. Cars ___
b. Bicycles ___
c. Scooters ___

G. Observe 10 customers arriving or departing the store.
a. by foot? ___
b. by bike? ___
c. by scooter? ___
d. by car? ___
e. by transit? ___
