What's next?

The Netherlands is frequently cited as an ideal living environment because cycling has maintained a sizable portion of the country's mobility. Internationally renowned researchers in a variety of academic fields are also coming to understand this more and more. Check out the University of Amsterdam's course on coursera called "Unravelling the Cycling City" for more information, and establish relationships with experts and people who share your interests to learn more.
