Information

People frequently use rental bikes for efficient transportation inside Amsterdam. Amsterdam's primary mode of transit is biking, thus it would be fascinating for urban planners to observe how dependent people are on bikes. The success of the ov-fiets system can be seen by the urban planners for themselves, and they can see how they are easily accessible in most stations in Amsterdam so that people can conveniently come and borrow or leave their ov-fiet bike for convenience.

On an OV-fiets, people can enjoy the independence of finishing their journey. To quickly discover how many bicycles are available at the station of their choice, they can just check the NS app. You can rent an OV-fiets from various stations for just € 4.45 per day per bicycle, making it a well-known affordable choice for renting bicycles.
