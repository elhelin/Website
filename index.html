Welcome to the Amsterdam Bike Scavenger Hunt!

The immersive experience to unravel the cycling city

50 years ago

Amsterdam's streets were congested with cars, and mass motorization was seen as the traveling mode of the future.

Entire neighbourhoods and existing bicycle infrastructure was removed to make room for cars. Cyclists were at great risk of being pushed out of Dutch cities.

Today

Amsterdam is equipped with an elaborate network of cycle paths and infrastructure that makes the bike the easiest mode of transport. Therefore the city is ruled by cyclists.
