Welcome to the Amsterdam Bike Scavenger Hunt!

The immersive experience to unravel the cycling city

50 years ago

Amsterdam's streets were congested with cars, and mass motorization was seen as the traveling mode of the future.

Entire neighbourhoods and existing bicycle infrastructure was removed to make room for cars. Cyclists were at great risk of being pushed out of Dutch cities.

Today

Amsterdam is well known for high rates of cycling. The city is equipped with an elaborate network of bike lanes and infrastructure that provides a safe, convenient and efficient mode of transport.

Cycling has become ingrained in the lives of many residents, fostering a healthier and more liveable community beyond just a means of travel.

What is it about?
